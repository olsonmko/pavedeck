How it works

Self-serve. Configurable. No sales call.

Sign up, connect your inventory feed, configure your templates. Pavedeck handles every vehicle from then on.

  1. Connect your inventory

    Pull from HomeNet, vAuto, DealerCenter, Frazer, AutoManager — or upload a CSV. We read; your existing systems keep working exactly as they do today.

  2. Configure your templates

    Pick your photo backdrop, your watermark, your VDP voice (professional, casual, performance-focused), your service area. One-time setup, ~20 minutes.

  3. Pavedeck runs every vehicle

    Every new VIN gets photos enhanced, VDP generated, pricing flagged. Output pushed back into your inventory system and website. Refreshed every 14 days to stay un-stale.
